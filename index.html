Research

Current — Active Research

Falsification of AI-enabled Cyber-Physical Systems

Developing a falsification technique that combines stochastic optimization and reinforcement learning to improve fault detection in AI-enabled CPS. Conducting empirical analysis to categorize CPS models and identify verification challenges specific to AI systems.

📍 University of Michigan-Dearborn 👩‍🏫 Supervisor: Prof. Khouloud Gaaloal 📅 Sept. 2024 – Present
CPS Safety & Verification
Completed · UM-D

Counterfactual-Guided Debugging of Cyber-Physical Systems (DeCaF)

Developed a framework for debugging CPS using counterfactual-guided explanations that identify minimal input changes preventing system failures. Evaluated across multiple Simulink CPS benchmarks.

👩‍🏫 Prof. Khouloud Gaaloal 📅 May – Aug. 2025 · Submitted
Completed · CMU Africa

Prosthetics Design for Low-Resource Environments

Improving access to customized prosthetics using smartphone-based digital reconstruction and data-driven prediction of mechanical functionality in resource-constrained communities.

👩‍🏫 Prof. Moise Busogi 📅 May 2023 – Aug. 2024
Completed · CMU Africa

Data-Driven Material Prediction for 3D Printing

Predicted material properties of 3D-printed parts using multi-layer perceptron (MLP) models linking specimen test results, printing conditions, and geometry. Generated and pre-processed simulation data under varying parameters.

👩‍🏫 Prof. Conrad Tucker 📅 Jan. – May 2024
Completed · CMU Africa

Adaptive Education for African Languages (ED-GPT)

Constructed an LLM pipeline for context-based answer generation for high school STEM subjects in Kinyarwanda. Achieved 98% accuracy with sentence similarity evaluation.

👩‍🏫 Prof. Azzizuddin Biyabani 📅 Oct. – Dec. 2023
Completed · CMU Africa

DTMF Demodulation using TinyML

Designed an experiment for accurate DTMF signal decoding using power spectral analysis. Captured and processed real-time signals on Arduino Nano Sense BLE and Raspberry Pi.

👩‍🏫 Prof. Mike Perkins 📅 May – Aug. 2023
